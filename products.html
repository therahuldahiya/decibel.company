High-Frequency Electronic Siren System

Our High-Frequency Electronic Siren System is designed for optimal sound projection, ensuring that alerts are heard clearly over long distances. Engineered for critical situations, it delivers timely warnings when every second counts.

Contact us

Output Power

Our systems deliver an impressive output of 120–145 dB, ensuring alerts are heard even in noisy environments. This powerful output is crucial for effective communication during emergencies.

Multi-Mode Communication

Our systems support a wide range of communication modes including IP, VHF/UHF, GSM/4G, and Satellite networks. This versatility ensures seamless operation across diverse infrastructures and in remote or disconnected regions.

Power Assurance

Designed for uninterrupted operation, our solution integrates solar power, battery backup, and grid connectivity. This hybrid setup guarantees 24x7 reliability, even in the most demanding conditions.

Rugged IP67 Enclosure

Built to withstand extreme environments, the IP67-rated enclosure protects against dust, water, and harsh weather. Ideal for coastal, industrial, or mountainous deployments, it ensures long-lasting durability.

Centralized & Distributed Control

Easily manage alerts and system operations through centralized NOC/EOC setups or distributed mobile applications. This flexible control architecture adapts to both routine monitoring and rapid emergency response.

CAP Protocol + GIS Maps

Our platform adheres to the Common Alerting Protocol (CAP) and integrates with GIS mapping tools. This ensures compatibility with national alerting systems and enhances situational awareness through visual geolocation.

Multiple Activation Modes

Activate alerts through multiple channels — local switches, remote commands, cloud-based dashboards, or automated triggers. This multi-layered activation ensures fast, reliable response no matter the scenario.
